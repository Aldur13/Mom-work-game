# How to Play

## Before the Game

### 1. Host creates a room
One person (the host) goes to the game website and enters their name, then clicks **Create a Room**. They receive a 4-letter room code.

### 2. Everyone joins
All players go to the same website on their own phone or laptop, enter their name, click **Join a Room**, and type the 4-letter code.

### 3. Fill in your profile
Every player — including the host — answers **10 personal questions** about themselves:
- What's your favorite food?
- What's your biggest pet peeve?
- What's your spirit animal?
- ... and 7 more

Take your time and be honest (or interesting). These answers become the questions everyone else has to guess!

### 4. Host starts the game
Once all players have submitted their profiles, the host's screen shows a **Start Game** button. Click it when you're ready.

---

## During the Game

### Each round works like this:

**Step 1 — The question appears**
> *"What is Sarah's answer to: What is your spirit animal?"*

Everyone except Sarah sees 4 answer choices. One is Sarah's real answer. The other three are **real answers from other players** to the same question.

**Step 2 — Everyone guesses**
You have **20 seconds**. Tap your answer as fast as you can — the faster you answer correctly, the more points you earn.

**Step 3 — Sarah watches**
Sarah (the "subject" this round) can see her own answer and watch how many people have guessed.

**Step 4 — The reveal**
When time runs out (or everyone answers), the correct answer is revealed along with:
- Who got it right ✓
- Who got it wrong ✗
- Points earned this round
- Updated leaderboard

**Step 5 — Host clicks Next Round**
The host advances to the next question. Repeat for 20 rounds.

---

## After the Game

A final podium screen shows the top 3 players and full rankings. To play again, everyone refreshes and creates a new room.

---

## Tips

- **Be specific in your profile answers** — vague answers like "chicken" are less fun than "spicy Korean fried chicken"
- **Discuss after each reveal** — the game is more fun when people react out loud
- **Play 2 rounds** if you have time — the second game is always better once everyone knows each other's answers
