## What is Know Your Crew?

Players fill in a personal profile before the game starts — their favorite food, spirit animal, hidden talent, and more. During gameplay, one player's answer is on the spot and everyone else races to guess it correctly.

The twist: the wrong-answer options are **real answers from other players** to the same question. So when you see "pickles and peanut butter" as a choice, you know exactly who wrote that.

**It's social, it's competitive, and it will teach you things about your coworkers you never expected.**
